# Choosing Your Language

Proxytrace can display its interface in more than one language. Today you can choose between
**English** and **German (Deutsch)**, and more languages may be added over time. Your choice is
personal: it is saved to your account and follows you across devices and browsers.

## Switching language

1. Open the **account menu** from the avatar button in the top-right corner.
2. Under **Language**, pick the language you want. A check mark shows the active one.
3. The interface switches immediately — there's no need to reload.

Your selection is remembered, so the next time you sign in (on any device) Proxytrace comes up in
the language you chose. Before you've made a choice, Proxytrace follows your browser's preferred
language and falls back to English.

## What stays in English

Proxytrace is built for AI engineers, so common technical terms that read more naturally in English
are **kept in English on purpose** — for example *Tool*, *User*, *Assistant*, *Trace*, *Token*,
*Prompt*, *Agent*, *Suite*, *Evaluator*, and *Proposal*. Only the surrounding interface text is
translated, so the product stays consistent with the wider AI ecosystem and its documentation.

> The user manual itself is currently available in English only.
